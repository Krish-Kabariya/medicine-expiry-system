Apothecary Stock Intelligence is a comprehensive pharmacy management platform designed for pharmacists, hospital dispensaries, and healthcare providers. We help you track inventory, predict expiry risks, and manage stock with precision — so you can focus on patient care.

What We Do
Our system provides real-time inventory tracking, automated expiry alerts, and intelligent forecasting to minimize wastage and maximize efficiency. From batch-level monitoring to supplier return management, every feature is built to solve real pharmacy challenges.

Whether you manage a single retail pharmacy or a multi-location hospital dispensary, Apothecary gives you the tools to maintain compliance, reduce losses from expired stock, and ensure critical medicines are always available when patients need them.
